Rain Spencer Family

Rain Spencer’s dad is Chauncey Browning Spencer, who died on July 15, 2020, aged 45, in an auto accident in North Carolina. After his death, Rain penned a heartfelt post on Instagram sharing pictures of him and mourning the loss. She wrote, “Too soon Dad. Love you unconditionally. Visit me in my dreams. Raise hell in heaven if that’s a real thing. Love you.”

The mother of The Summer I Turned Pretty actress, Carla Cherry, 48, has been working as a Digital Marketing Consultant at Carla Cherry Productions since 2008. In her LinkedIn handle and Facebook, she claims she helps coaches & consultants LEVEL UP inside a gamified cohort.

From May 2017 to March 2018, Carla was also a senior producer at Ovation LLC. There, she produced Inside Versailles, Ovation TV’s exclusive after-show for the hit-period drama “Versailles.” Carla has a BA in Film from New York University.

Rain shares a close relationship with her mother. On Mother’s Day (May 14, 2018), Rain took to Instagram to share a couple of old pictures of herself and her mother and captioned it, “Happy mamas day. This woman has saved my ass so many times. The strongest, most brutally honest, hilarious, and beautiful person inside and out. So much love and light.”

In an interview, Rain once shared that she would spend most of her childhood summers with her grandpa. Her grandfather lived on a boat for 12 years and lived in different places, and so did Rain. They spend a lot of time in Panama, Colombia, and Honduras. Reportedly, Rain’s grandmother took her on dreamy trips; they took trains through Europe, went skiing, and visited New York.

Does Rain Spencer Have A Sister?

Yes, Rain Spencer has two sisters, namely Kyra and Isabella Spencer. In 2023, Isabella resided in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina.

What Is Actress Rain Spencer Real Name?

Reportedly, Rain Spencer’s real name is Nina Rain Spencer.

How Much Is Rain Spencer Net Worth?

As of 2023, Rain Spencer’s net worth was estimated to be under $150 thousand.

Talking about her acting career, Rain started doing theatres when she was 5. “The desire to act was intuitive for me and it allowed me to express myself freely without feeling judged- especially as a teenager,” she told LadyGunn.

Rain’s first real acting role came in 2010 when she was the face of the United States for the US Pavilion at the World’s Fair in China. As she said, she completed her filming in Los Angeles and then flew out for the Opening and Closing Ceremonies. There, she signed autographs, met with Hilary Clinton, and had bodyguards.

In the same year, Rain also acted in two short films, namely The Garden and The Talking Head. In 2017, she played a Young Girl in the TV series called The Super Man followed by a lead role as Jane in the movie Good Girl Jane in 2022. Good Girl Jane premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival in New York City and Rain attended the event.

Rain played her breakthrough role of Taylor in four episodes of The Summer I Turned Pretty in 2022. Her role in The Summer I Turned Pretty was the complete opposite of Jane in Good Girl Jane. “If they were in a room together, they would absolutely hate each other,” Rain once said in an interview.

Rain is represented by DPN Talented Commercial and VO and Range Media Partners.

Rain Spencer Height
