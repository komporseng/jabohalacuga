Bow Wow's girlfriend flaunts her curvaceous figure in tiny black swimsuit in new photos

July 01, 2024
blog

Kiyomi Leslie, best known for being rapper Bow Wow’s new girlfriend, took to Instagram to share a photo of her beautiful figure.

In the image, the woman was wearing a black swimsuit and a matching see-through pool robe while facing all the way around. Her hands were placed under the upper part of her bathing suit, which gave the impression that she was about to take it off.

Even though the woman’s long hair and clothing were outstanding, Kiyomi’s body was the highlight of the post. Her strong and fit figure attracted a lot of attention from people who rushed to the comment section to share their thoughts.

One of them was Instagram user Damion Johnson, who confessed that it had been the best picture he had ever seen.

According to Rolling Out, Bow Wow and Kiyomi were first spotted together at the season 2 premiere of Growing up Hip Hop at the Woodruff Arts Center in Atlanta earlier this year.

After that, the rapper made their relationship official by sharing a few photos of both of them hanging out together on social media, including a photo uploaded to Instagram showing both of them backstage at the Trumpet Awards.

Bow Wow said that he was there just to show his support for Jermaine Dupri, who just rekindled his romance with Janet Jackson, as he was being acknowledged during the ceremony.

More recently, in March 2018, Bow Wow took to Twitter to share a photo and a message to honor his girlfriend on her birthday.

After wishing her a happy ‘born day,’ the rapper said that he would make sure she had the best birthday ever.

He finished by urging her to pack her bags as he would take her to the islands during the weekend.

At the moment, the couple is pleased about spending time with each other and, even though there are several rumors about a secret marriage, they have not made anything official.
