Early Life

Alicia Aylies was born on 21 April 1998 and she is currently 24 years old. Alicia hails from Fort-de-France, Martinique, France, and her zodiac sign is Taurus. Talking about her family, Alicia’s parents are mother Marie-Chantal Belfroy and father Philippe Aylies.

Likewise, Alicia’s parents are Martiniquais. While Alicia’s father is an environmental manager, her mother is a driving school instructor. Moreover, Alicia is an only child.

She moved with her mother to Matoury, French Guiana after her parents separated when she was only 2 years old.

Regarding her educational journey, Alicia attended school in Remire-Montjoly. After graduating from lycée with a degree in science in the year 2016, Alicia attended the University of French Guiana where she pursued law.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Alicia is a model and pageant winner. Alicia was the titleholder of Miss France in the year 2017. The same year, Alicia also represented France at Miss Universe 2017.

Talking about the beginning of her career, Alicia started off as a model with Mannky’n. It is a Guyanese agency. Alicia then represented French Guiana at Miss France 2017 pageant.

Subsequently, on 17 December 2016, Miss France 2016 Iris Mittenaere crowned Alicia Miss France in Montpellier.

Likewise, as Miss France 2017, Alicia represented France at the Miss Universe 2017 pageant. Besides being a model and pageant titleholder, Alicia has traveled around France as a competitive fencer.

Similarly, Alicia ventured to music by joining Rude Empire Music Group in February 2021.
