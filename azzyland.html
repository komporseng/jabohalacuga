No information about her parents and their names has been available until now. Like many other famous personalities, she also wants to keep her personal life private. However, she has a sister who is also a gamer but her name is not available at the moment.

Professional Life

She was first introduced to gaming by her father and uncle and she grew up watching them play video games. She was introduced to the game, ‘The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time’ at the age of seven, and eventually that turned out to be a life-changing experience for her.

As she grew up, she decided to make a career out of gaming. She quit her medical studies and focused more on her gaming and YouTube career. Then, she started experimenting with cosplays. In her first cosplay, she represented a character called Vegeta from the famous Japanese animated series, ‘Dragon Ball Z‘. Azzy stated in one of her videos that her all-time favorite cosplay character is Zelda from the video game series, ‘The Legend of Zelda‘.

She created her individual YouTube channel ‘Azzyland‘ on May 8, 2015, and has garnered 4,326,492,412 views until now. She created the channel to post her cosplay videos. Likewise, She filmed all her cosplay activities and posted them on her YouTube channel. Her videos were instantly popular and took the internet by storm. Later on, she was soon seen participating as an official cosplay artist in almost all the conventions around the world.

Unfortunately, the incident made her the subject of many memes and there was even a public backlash against her. Later, she posted an apology video which was appreciated by FaZe Jen.

Furthermore, she created a YouTube record by twerking continuously for 24 hours. This record helped her gain more subscribers. After setting the record for twerking, she made a promise to her fans that she would go nude when her channel musters a million subscribers. Surprisingly, she kept her promise and uploaded a video of her going nude on the street after she crossed a million subscribers. Furthermore, she also posts reactions and prank videos on her channel.

Azzy announced on her Twitter account that she made a #FightTheFire donation. She contributed $10,000 to the charity to help the devastating Australian Wildfires in 2020. She also donated $6000 to the Make-A-Wish 2018 Rope For Hope Charity to help sick kids where the charity had her ripple down the side of a skyscraper in Toronto to raise money.  Moreover, she is a PADI-certified Scuba driver.

Furthermore, she starred in an episode in Season 2 of My 90-Year-Old Roommate back in 2016. Likewise, her friends and fellow YouTubers state that Azzy is a very shy person and her music video is a “big step for her”.
