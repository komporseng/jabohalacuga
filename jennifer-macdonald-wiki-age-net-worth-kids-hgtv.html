A new home renovation show Houses with History has dropped on HGTV on 29 September 2021.

This series is a look at houses and barns built by early Americans and the fascinating backstories behind these oldest homes.

Houses with History stars a trio of Massachusetts Jennifer MacDonald, Rich Soares, and Mike Lemieux who have dedicated their lives to historic real estate.

With that being the introduction, we now are about to explore everything about Jen MacDonald, her age, net worth, kids, her experience with Houses with History, and more in this Jennifer MacDonald Wiki.

Jennifer MacDonald On MTV’s Houses With History

In each episode of Houses with History a history buff, carpenter, and a designer work together to save century-old properties. Jennifer MacDonald among these three is a designer. Although she is only called “Jen” on the show, her full name is Jennifer MacDonald. Then, she is also known as Jenny.

Excited about the show’s release Jennifer was counting the days before the world could see her with a keen eye for detail and amazing ideas to turn a house into a home, whilst still preserving its natural history and aura.

If you do not already know, Jennifer stars on the show together with her husband Mike Lemieux. Yes, the history buff in the trio we mentioned earlier.

Jennifer and Mike, as the owners of Full Circle Homes, had spoken to WBZ-TV in 2020 for the station’s It Happen Here series. For that, they had saved and restored dozens of antique homes on the South Shore. And exactly at that point, their ability to renovate and preserve historic spaces was recognized by their fellow homie and HGTV star, Jonathan Knight.

This way, Houses with History came out and the six-episode series was filmed during the pandemic.

Jennifer MacDonald And Mike Lemieux’s Relationship

So, in short, they were first friends, fell in love, and realized they both had a passion for real estate and all old things. Thereafter they also became business partners.
