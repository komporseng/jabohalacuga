Caroline Ribeiro is a Brazilian supermodel, presenter, and actress who is distinguished for walking the ramp at the Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show for 3 consecutive years. Besides that, she has also walked for many high-end brands like Chloé, Chanel, Dolce & Gabbana, Christian Dior, Gucci, Calvin Klein, Louis Vuitton, Givenchy, Tommy Hilfiger, and Versace. Her tall physique and eye candy appearance also made her muse for several designers like Valentino, Alberta Ferretti, Jean-Paul Gaultier, Diane von Furstenberg, Michael Kors, and Alexander McQueen. The veteran model also earned her a place on the cover of several highly regarded magazines likes Harper’s Bazaar, Vogue, Elle, Monet Magazines, and L’Officiel. She has also made appearances in various TV shows and films like Anjo de Mim (1996), Belíssima (2005), and Lower City (2005). With time she has also amassed a huge fan base with more than 100k followers on Instagram and over 100k followers on Twitter.

    Caroline Ribeiro Facts

  • She grew up in Belém, the Amazonian region of Brazil.
  • Caroline found her passion for modeling after she had won the Brazilian edition of the Dakota-Elite Model Look Contest in the past. Then, she moved to São Paulo to work upon her modeling.
  • Her career took off after she was spotted by designer Tom Ford just as she was giving up on her passion for modeling. With his help, she made her breakthrough at the VH1/Vogue Fashion Awards in 1991, where she walked for Gucci.
  • Caroline was featured in the 2001 Pirelli Calendar.
  • In 2001, she signed a $6 million contract with Revlon and took the place of American model Cindy Crawford after she stepped down in February that year.
  • She walked at the Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show in 2000, 2001, and 2002.
  • In 2008, Caroline was hired by MTV to present their show Archive Luau MTV. She also presented the IT MTV show from 2009 to 2012.
  • In 2016, she started working as a presenter for TNT Brasil alongside blogger Hugo Gloss. With TNT, she covered some of the biggest events such as the Oscar, SAG Awards, and the Golden Globes.
  • She has worked with some of the best photographers like Steven Meisel and Mario Testino.
