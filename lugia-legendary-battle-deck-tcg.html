Lugia Legendary Battle Deck

Package art

The Lugia Legendary Battle Deck is one of a pair of Theme Decks released on May 5, 2017 centered around the tower duo. It focuses on and -type Pokémon. Like other Theme Decks, it includes a two-player playmat, a rule sheet, damage counters, a Lugia deckbox, a Lugia coin, a quick guide to using the deck, and a TCGO code card.

Description

Get Ready for a Legendary Battle!
Befriend the Diving Pokémon Lugia with the Pokémon TCG: Legendary Battle Deck! This 60-card deck is ready to play right away, with powerful cards and winning strategies worthy of an ace Trainer. Let the battle begin!

Information

The Lugia Legendary Battle Deck focuses on Ancient Origins Lugia-EX, as well as other Pokémon from the Black & White and early XY Series. The deck includes exclusive versions of a number of cards:

  • Sheen Holofoil print (×2) of Ancient Origins Lugia-EX (previously Mirror Holofoil)
  • Non Holofoil print (×2) of Dark Explorers Blissey (previously Tinsel Holofoil)
  • Non Holofoil print (×1) of Dragons Exalted Sigilyph (previously Tinsel Holofoil)
  • Glossy, Sheen Holofoil print (×8) of Psychic Energy
