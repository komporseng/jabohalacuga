Experiencing Jealousy: Prince Harry Opens Up about Seeing Chelsy Davy at the Royal Wedding

June 28, 2024
blog

Prince Harry has revealed that he experienced feelings of “jealousy” upon witnessing other men showing interest in his former girlfriend, Chelsy Davy, at Prince William and Princess Kate’s Wedding in 2011.

Harry and Chelsy had an on-and-off relationship from 2004 to 2010 after meeting in South Africa.

In his book “Spare”, the Duke of Sussex reminisced about their encounter, where he described Chelsy as different and uninterested in his title, unlike many others he had met.

Their relationship became serious, but they faced challenges when Harry was in the army.

He recounted a difficult conversation where she felt unseen and he felt taken aback by her understanding of his detachment.

Ultimately, their different desires for a public life led to them parting ways, but they crossed paths again at Prince William and Kate Middleton’s wedding.

Harry admitted that seeing Chelsy at the wedding stirred up suppressed feelings and he felt jealousy toward the men vying for her attention.

Despite their romantic history, Harry and Chelsy remained friends, and she even attended his wedding to Meghan Markle.
